# CIA TRIAD
## CIA triad represents : Confidentiality, Integrity, and Availibility
CIA is a type of basic cybersecurity model. CIA triad guides how organization protect data, network and systems from theats and attacks.

### Confidentiality 
it ensures that sensitive information is not accessible to all. 
To manitain confidentiality various methods are employed like encryption, authentication, acesscontrol etc.

### Integrity
it guarantees that data remains accurate and consistant by using methods like hashing, logging, digital signatures etc
The goal of integrety is to prevent tampering and corruption of data.

### Availibility 
Authorized personales must be albe to acess these encryted data any where they need.
the main goal is to keep backup and to protect against outages or attacks
